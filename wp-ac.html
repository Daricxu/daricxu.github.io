Whirlpool Major Project

GLOBAL SORCERER AC PROJECT

Air conditioner is a very "big" category for Whirlpool’s OEM business. It's main target market is Brazil and India with OEM suppliers all based in China. Goal of this project is to design a medium-level winning split air-conditioner for Indian, Brazilian and European Market; The new design should fit for OEM supplier’s capability but with consumer-attracting feature.
