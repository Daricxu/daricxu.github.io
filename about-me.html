About Me
Resume
I use design as an opportunity of exploring unknown facts and creating future options. Facts of behavior pattern and user preference are the director of my pen, paper and mouse for design.
I started my design career with a motivation that designers can work with new stuff everyday and their lives are full of passion.
As a designer, I realized my favorite is to connect the dots in life or research and fabricate them on paper into design ideas. I'm fascinate by the epiphanies of figuring out what users are expecting. Form, shape, material, data visualization and interaction are my things of design.
I tried hard to learn every story behind people's preference which I always believe is reasonable and predictable. The quicker I can build test my idea, the more chance my design will succeed. I'm a big dreamer of breakthrough design involving both hardware, software and cloud service for our domestic life.
Here is my journey from one job to another, from one culture to another:

Almost eight years in Haier, I grew from a  ‘green’ student to a productive designer, design manager. Consultants from Japan and Korea educated and influenced me a lot in our daily co-working experience. I built my own way of ideation and discovering story from user‘s perspective. With Haier’s fast project pace, I participate or lead more than 70 design projects covering refrigerator, washer, air-conditioner, water heater, microwave oven, range hood, cooktop, TV, SDA, etc.
